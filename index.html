Welcome to My 'Liste De Courses' App

'Liste De Courses' is an Android Application. With this App, you can add products you want to buy in a list, list you can share with other people if you are doing shopping with them. Bought products are deleted from your list and automatically deleted as well from lists you share.

Shops, radius and products

Theses will be added to the main database with a back office Qt application. With this, users will only have to select a product from a particular radius and just add it to his/her shopping list.
